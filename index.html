Buldak Noodles

What are Buldak Noodles?

Buldak noodles, also known as fire noodles, are a popular Korean instant noodle dish known for their extremely spicy flavor.

History

Buldak noodles were first introduced by Samyang Foods in South Korea in 2012. They quickly gained popularity for their intense heat and became a viral sensation on social media platforms.

Key Ingredients

  • Spicy seasoning sauce
  • Ramen noodles
  • Optional toppings: sesame seeds, seaweed, green onions, cheese, boiled egg

Spice Level

Buldak noodles are famous for their intense heat, typically measured in Scoville heat units (SHU). They often range from 4,000 to 8,000 SHU, making them one of the spiciest instant noodles available.

How to Cook Buldak Noodles

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add the ramen noodles and cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Drain the noodles and mix in the spicy seasoning sauce.
  4. Add optional toppings as desired.
  5. Enjoy the fiery flavor of Buldak noodles!

Health Benefits

While Buldak noodles are known for their spiciness, they also offer some health benefits. The capsaicin in spicy foods like Buldak noodles may boost metabolism, reduce inflammation, and improve heart health.
